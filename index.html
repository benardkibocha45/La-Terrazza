    Italian Soul, African Canvas

    Founded in 2018, La Terrazza blends authentic Italian cuisine with Nairobi's vibrant art scene.

    • Rooftop dining with panoramic city views
    • Rotating exhibitions featuring East African artists
    • Handmade pasta and wood-fired pizzas using imported Italian ingredients

    Our space at Promenade Mall has become a cultural hub where food lovers and art enthusiasts gather. From our signature seafood risotto to monthly artist showcases, every visit offers a new experience.

    Art Gallery

    Current Exhibition

    "Tuscan Skies meets Savannah"

    Featuring works by contemporary Kenyan artists inspired by Italian-African cultural exchange.

    This month's exhibition showcases 12 emerging artists exploring themes of migration, flavor, and landscape through mixed media. All artworks are available for purchase with 20% of proceeds supporting local art education initiatives.
